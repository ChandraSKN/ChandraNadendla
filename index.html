About Me

Innovative and results-driven Product Manager with a strong foundation in Mechanical Engineering, Robotics, and Product Design. After earning my MSc from the University of Glasgow, I’ve led R&D at ARC and shaped product strategy at Trinnovate. Over the past 4+ years, I’ve combined CAD, 3D modelling, and advanced simulation tools (ANSYS, Abaqus) with full-stack development (Angular, React, AWS, Azure) to deliver cost-effective, manufacturable solutions.

  • 4+ Years experience in working across different domains: served in large firms like Cognizant, various startups in diverse roles, and contributed to content creation for companies.
  • Leadership Roles: Product Manager at Trinnovate; Business Development Executive at Geo Lands Doctor; Supervisor at Morrisons
  • Technical Expertise: CAD & FEA (ANSYS, Abaqus); Additive Manufacturing; Angular, React, Python, MATLAB; AWS & Azure, Lean Manufacturing
